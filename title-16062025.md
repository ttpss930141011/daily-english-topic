## 2. Learning Points:

### 2.1 Common Colloquial Phrases & Idioms:
- gonna (going to): informal future marker. E.g., 'I’m gonna join you later.'
- mess up: to make a mistake. E.g., 'I completely messed up my presentation.'
- hit the nail on the head: describe something exactly right. E.g., 'You hit the nail on the head with that answer.'

### 2.2 Abbreviations & Internet Slang:
- IMO (in my opinion): used when giving personal views.
- TMI (too much information): when someone overshares.
- SMH (shaking my head): expressing disbelief or disappointment.

### 2.3 Key Grammar & Sentence Patterns:
- Present perfect for experiences:
  • Pattern: Have you ever + past participle?
  • Example: 'Have you ever shared an embarrassing story online?'
- So… that…  for cause and effect:
  • Pattern: so + adjective + that + result
  • Example: 'I was so tired that I fell asleep at my desk.'
